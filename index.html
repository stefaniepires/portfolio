About
Me

Do you ever get a rush of excitement when you come across a website that has a clean but eye-catching design and is user-friendly? I do, too! This is what brought me to web design, however I spent over a decade just fantasizing about how amazing it would be to have the skills to build mesmorizing websites that are responsive. I used to spend hours working with templates for businesses that I wanted to start. Then, I realized I didn't have a passion for the business I supposedly wanted to start. I have a passion for building websites and apps!

This realization led me to spend countless of hours on google and online courses, which eventually pushed me to enroll in a Bootcamp at the University of Pennsylvania. In a short amount of time, I have gained and will continously gain the skills I have always longed to have.

In short, I am a Full Stack Developer with a passion for designing attractive and user-friendly applications. Below I have highlighted some of my projects as well as have links to my LinkedIn and Github profiles.
